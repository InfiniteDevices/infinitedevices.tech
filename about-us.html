History

Milestones of Infinite Devices

April 2018

Our Humble Beginnings

On a flight from Germany to San Francisco the initial two founders drafted the idea of a independent IoT platform, integrated AI and blockchain for ODM signature patterns.

May 2018

A Company is Born

We built our first website, coded a blockchain for device registry and finally our MQTT bridge. We got a Google Cloud Startup grant with GCP credits to startup our SaaS.

December 2018

infinimesh alpha release on GCP

Our first official code drop got viral, we had some recognition on Reddit and HackerNews. In the next weeks we got 30+ stars on GitHub and our idea started to attract developers and potential users.

March 2019

Incorporation USA

We incorporated as Infinimesh, Inc. in Delaware and opened our first office in Seattle, WA.

December 2019

IONOS and Infinite Devices

IONOS and Infinite Devices agreed on a close partnership to create a disruptive, GDPR conform cloud to overtake the US public cloud provider in IoT.

January 2020

Infinite Devices GmbH

We got officially registered as Infinite Devices GmbH and opened our headquater in Magdeburg, Germany. We started our private beta for infinimesh together with IONOS.
