infinimesh features

infinimesh is the state-of-the-art backbone for your entire IoT fleet

We care about Data

infinimesh's data privacy and security standards that fully adhere to European laws (GDPR/DSGVO), US privacy protection laws (CCPA) and Gaia X compatibility make infinimesh the perfect Open Source solution for your cloud, datacenter, and desktop. Infinimesh runs on Kubernetes, the most stable and state-of-the-art container orchestration platform.

infinimesh provides the most advanced experience to safely growth your business:

Data Security and GDPR

Keep your customer data secure and free your data from US cloud services and its underlying US cloud act. We use military-grade encryption standards to guarantee secure message transmission. Our MQTT broker accepts only certificates which are known by the signed CA and makes sure that not yet known, but trusted devices can send data into a dedicated secure queue.

Simple Device Management

infinimesh offers a powerful but simple framework to visualize clusters of devices within your organization and set permissions up to device level. Use real-time and two-way correspondence for each of your devices in your fleet and sphere. Device statistics based on time series, machine learning, and AI offer detailed insights into your devices, detect anomalies and respond accordingly.

Full scalability

Our highly-scalable backend can power millions of devices at your fingertips. Whether you run a few hundred or millions of devices, infinimesh is fully scalable from your desktop to the datacenter, from your notebook to the cloud. Infinimesh runs everywhere – in a managed cloud or in your own infrastructure.

Minimum TCO

We acknowledge that in the world of IoT only scalability on lowest TCO matters. Our highly-scalable backend powers millions of devices at minimum total cost of ownership. You have full cost control as your device fleet grows. We guarantee constant development without additional or hidden costs.

Seamless integration

Infinimesh offers straight forward datasharing for AI and ML procedures. No matter if you are building smart products or leverage IoT devices to improve your processes, infinimesh integrates seamlessly into your software landscape. Connecting your IoT data into your backend systems like SAP®, IBM Watson IoT®, Salesforce® or every other enterprise system has never been easier.

Full transparency

Infinimesh is Open Source. The transparency and “hands on” nature of Open Source offers much flexibility and makes infinimesh highly sustainable.
Installing infinimesh IoT takes you less than 20 minutes. Maintenance and updates are managed by the Kubernetes operator which saves valuable time in operations and maintenance.

infinimesh IoT core principles

Asset Management

infinimesh gives you quick and deep insights about all IoT assets managed - be they managed over gateways, edge devices or directly connected - never lose any information what your fleet is doing! IoT and IIoT operation at scale require transparency and insights about and into the managed fleet. infinimesh has developed a graph-based organizing framework with real-time device statistics. Arrange your devices in an overview which reflects your organizational or project based set-up, manage which device belongs where, manage groups on different layers and define who has access as RBAC model. To make this fully secure, we provide granular access management up to device level. IoT operations have never been easier!

IoT Security

Building new IoT or IIoT products also needs to have manageable security mechanisms in place. Starting at the factory provisioning, knowing which device was built with the right certificate is mandatory for strategic implementations. Trust is the most important factor in a valuable IoT business chain. See what the infinimesh IoT Platform can provide and help to build up a trusted fleet of devices. We use military-grade encryption standards to guarantee secure message transmission, even over uncertain and unstable WAN connectivity like LoRa, SigFox, WhiteNoise or Satellite. Our MQTT broker accepts only certificates which are known by the signed CA and make sure that not yet known, but trusted devices can send data into a dedicated secure queue - never lose any information during long rollout phases.

Simplicity

One characteristic of the Industrial IoT is the development of smart products, sensors and actors. Operating and maintaining smart products entails new complex infrastructures, concepts and operational models. 
Installing infinimesh IoT takes you less than 20 minutes. Maintenance and updates are managed by the Kubernetes Operator which saves valuable time in operations and maintenance. No matter if you are building smart products or leverage IoT devices to improve your processes, infinimesh integrates seamlessly into your software development landscape. Use either a managed cloud or install infinimesh in your own infrastructure. infinimesh runs everywhere, if you want even without Kubernetes.
