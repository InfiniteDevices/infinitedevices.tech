Your IoT – Our Expertise

Infinite devices is the development company for IoT solutions integrating the infinimesh Open Source platform. infinimesh allows for the seamless integration of your entire IoT ecosystem independently of any cloud technology and provider. Manage millions of devices using our fully scalable, portable, and cost efficient platform without any vendor lock-ins.

infinimesh's data privacy and security standards that fully adhere to European laws (GDPR/DSGVO), US privacy protection laws (CCPA) and Gaia X compatibility make infinimesh the perfect Open Source solution for your cloud, datacenter, and desktop. infinimesh runs on Kubernetes, the most stable and state-of-the-art container orchestration platform.

Infinite Possibilities - infinimesh IoT

Smart Mobility & Transportation

Connected and self-driving cars, advanced robots, autonomous drones, and interconnected railroads – infinimesh can power all of them. We solve the greatest challenge of this automated and interconnected world: Reliable and secure data management. The upcoming communication revolution (5G, IIoT, NFRB) enables real-time (RTS) data-processing in complex networks. infinimesh enables real data-driven business models and gives the confidence to manage large and costly assets in a cost-efficient and reliable way. Built for low latency, data QoS and high data ingest, infinimesh is the workhorse for everything you want to build.

Smart Health & Smart Care

The future of diagnosis, nursing and medication - all fields of healthcare will prosper in the light of linking IoT and AI to the human body. Once connected, data can be exchanged, and the body and device can be remotely monitored and controlled. This revolution is already influencing daily practices for patients and professionals. The patient is empowered and more active through digital devices. infinimesh facilitates the next level of smart health by offering a platform for reliable and secure management of patient data. The platform increases the pace for medical studies and health sciences, finally instituting the „Internet of Bodies“.

Smart City & Infrastructure

Smart cities, smart grids and intelligent infrastructure are the backbone of the upcoming energy transition. infinimesh makes your long-lasting assets smart by combining ML and AI technology without driving cost or complexity. Monitoring low-voltage grids, managing high-voltage transformators, performing complex switch scenarios or operate flexible street lightning in a new way – the platform excels equally driven by the team’s vast experience in utility and energy distribution companies. infinimesh enables fraction and hassle-free data transmission even with disturbed data connection lines like LTE, 5G, LoRa or SigFox.

Smart Renewables & Clean Energy

Renewable energy generation systems like windmills, solar farms, biomass or water assets generate large amounts of telemetry data. Reaping the benefits of these data using machine learning and AI technology to build new distribution channels requires a new operating and management model. Ranging from predictive maintenance, realtime workload estimations, CO2 neutrality to added value services, customer inclusion and interoperability – infinimesh IoT enables real data-driven business models, increase your margin and interface transition in your bussiness.
