Our Mission: 
Organize the connected world and shape the information for all


Infinite Device's software engineers develop the next-generation IoT platform and technology that will change how billions of devices connect and interact with information and one another. Our products need to handle information at massive scale and build the spine of the upcoming interconnected revolution.
We're looking for people who bring fresh ideas from all areas, including information management, large-scale system design, multi-path networking and streaming data storage concepts, IT and IoT security, artificial intelligence, UI design and mobile UX design - and so forth. We don’t adapt cutting edge, we’re building it. Including the latest software for kubernetes and beyond. As an Infinite Devices engineer, you will work on specific projects critical to Infinite Devices’s needs with opportunities to switch teams and projects as you and our fast-paced business grow and evolve. We need our engineers to be versatile, display leadership qualities and be enthusiastic to take on new problems across the full-stack as we continue to push the technology forward.
